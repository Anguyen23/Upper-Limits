What is Parkour?

Parkour: noun. The sport of moving along a route, typically in a city, trying to get through various obstacles in the quickest and most efficient manner possible, as by jumping, climbing, or running.

Misconceptions about Parkour:

  • Parkour & Freerunning are the same
  • Flips in the City
  • Useless during a Zombie Apocalypse
